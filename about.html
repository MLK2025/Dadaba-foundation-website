About Dadaba Foundation

Who We Are

Together, We Make
Lasting Change

Dadaba Foundation is the philanthropic arm of Dadaba Group. We work hand-in-hand with local leaders to deliver practical, measurable solutions—drilling boreholes and teaching WASH, funding girls’ STEM education, running mobile health outreaches, and supporting climate-smart farming and youth jobs. Every project is designed for community ownership, long-term maintenance, and real impact.
