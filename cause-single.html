Clinics: Equipment & Supplies

Healthcare

Across Ghana, many community facilities operate without the basic tools they need. Dadaba Foundation partners with district health teams to provide priority equipment—BP monitors, delivery beds, fetal dopplers, neonatal warmers, autoclaves—and trains staff on safe usage and maintenance.

Your support helps us equip rural CHPS compounds and clinics so mothers, newborns and people with chronic conditions can access timely, quality care close to home.

What Your Gift Makes Possible

• Essential diagnostics for early detection and referral.
• Safe deliveries with sterilisation and emergency kits.
• Cold-chain and storage for vaccines and medicines.
• Refresher training for nurses and midwives.

Where We’re Working

Current focus districts include parts of Bono East, Northern, and Volta Regions, prioritising facilities with the highest patient loads and equipment gaps identified by local health authorities.
